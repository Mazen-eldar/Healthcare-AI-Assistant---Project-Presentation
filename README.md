##  **Problem Overview**

Traditional healthcare systems suffer from:

* Slow & manual clinic onboarding
* Unorganized clinic management
* Lack of unified scheduling & appointment booking
* Weak authentication and no 2FA security
* Patients struggling to find specialties, clinics, doctors, and available slots
* No AI support to guide patients or simplify the booking experience

---

##  **Our Solution**

A scalable, modular backend providing:

* Central authentication with OTP login & 2FA
* Full clinic onboarding workflow (approve / reject / activate)
* Clinic management panel for staff, doctors, schedules, pricing & branding
* Automated appointment slot generation
* Patient browsing for specialties, clinics, doctors & available schedules
* Integrated *AI Assistant* for guidance, recommendations & booking support

---

##  **Authentication & Security**

Our auth module includes:

* User Registration + Email Verification
* Login with OTP
* Optional 2FA setup
* Secure password reset with OTP
* Refresh token & token rotation
* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

---

##  **System Roles**

* **System Admin** → Approves clinic requests & manages entire platform
* **Clinic Admin** → Manages clinic after approval
* **Clinic Users** → Receptionists / assistants inside each clinic
* **Doctors** → Assigned schedules & appointment slots
* **Patients** → Browse, view availability & book appointments

---

##  **Core Process Flow**

1. Clinics submit registration request
2. System Admin reviews & approves
3. Approved clinics become active
4. Clinic Admin adds doctors & staff
5. Schedules & appointment slots are created
6. Patients register & log in securely
7. Patients browse → specialties → clinics → doctors
8. View available times & book appointments
9. AI Assistant supports the entire patient journey
